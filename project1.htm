Website Critical Questions:

  1. What was your first impression of this site?
  2. How does this site organize the flow of information?
  3. Does the site structure make sense or does it confuse you? Why?
  4. Does the site's graphics/layout/multimedia enhance or detract from it? Why?
  5. How long did it take for the home page to load? Was the load time reasonable?
  6. Was the text color and size readable? Did the background enhance or detract?
  7. Did the site have a navigation bar or other navigation tool? Was it well done?
  8. Did the site have a consistent color scheme/look across all pages?
  9. How does the site respond to smaller window sizes (responsive design)?
  10. What does this site offer that was unusual or very well done?

Answers

My first impression of this site is that it is very simple and minimalistic.

This site organizes it's information flow using a top navigation bar with the main links like cakes, pastries, viennoiserie, bread, savoury, macarons and contact . The homepage has a centred layout with a picture of the locationof the store and some text sections under it .

The site's structure is clear and easy to follow. The links are grouped in the top bar which makes it easy to follow.

The site's graphics layout multimedia enhance the site, because the simple layout and images makes it clear to know what we're looking for.

The homepage loaded quickly, under 2 seconds .The load time for this site is reasonable.

The text color and size were readable, if a background color or image was used it would enhance the experience of the website.

The site has a navigation bar at the top and remain consistent across the site, it has all the navigation tools needed .

The site has a white color backgroound that is repeated on all pages .

The site's respond to smaller window size is okay .

This site has done very well maintaining a simple and a minimal website that make it easy and friendly to access and navigate.
